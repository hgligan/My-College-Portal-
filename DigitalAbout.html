Introduction

Digital Marketing Essentials provides an overview of key concepts, channels, and tactics used to promote products and services online. From SEO to social media, learn how to build effective campaigns and measure success.

Key Topics

  • SEO (Search Engine Optimization): Optimizing websites for better search rankings.
  • Social Media Marketing: Leveraging platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter for engagement.
  • PPC (Pay-Per-Click) Advertising: Google Ads and targeted paid campaigns.
  • Content Marketing: Creating valuable content to attract and retain audiences.
  • Email Marketing: Building lists and sending personalized campaigns.
  • Analytics and Tools: Using Google Analytics, SEMrush, and more to track performance.
